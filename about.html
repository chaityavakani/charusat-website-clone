About CHARUSAT & The Student E-Portal

"Amritam Tu Vidya" (Knowledge is eternal)

Guided by this motto, CHARUSAT is dedicated to transforming society through the creation, augmentation, and dissemination of knowledge.

Our Journey

The Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT) was established in **2009** by the Charotar Moti Sattavis Patidar Kelavani Mandal. Built upon the foundation of the Education Campus Changa (ECC) dating back to 2000, our institution is committed to putting the Charotar region on the global education map.
