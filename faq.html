Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find quick answers to common questions about the e-Portal, academic procedures, and fees.

🔒 Account & Login

If you forget your password, navigate to the **Login Page** and click on the **'Forgot Password?'** link. You will be prompted to enter your registered Enrollment ID or email address to receive a password reset link.

New students must first complete the **Registration** process using the Provisional Admission Number provided by the administration. Your permanent Enrollment ID and initial password will be sent to your registered email address upon verification.

📊 Academic & Results

Results are typically declared **4 to 6 weeks** after the final examination date. Check the **Dashboard** under 'Recent Exam Grades' and the 'Academic Calendar' on the **Events Page** for official notification dates.

Your current attendance percentage for all subjects is displayed on your **Student Dashboard** under the 'Attendance Status' card. Detailed subject-wise breakdown is available via the link provided there.

💸 Fees & Payments

Login to your **Dashboard**, navigate to the 'Pending Fees Due' section, and click the link to the payment portal. We accept payment via Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking, and UPI.
